Who we are

OK AGENCIES, a trusted name in the electrical industry since 1985, was founded by Mr. V.A. Oak and continues to thrive under strong leadership, delivering expertise in Turnkey Project Execution, HTLT Switchgear Maintenance, and Electrical Contracting. With over 35+ years of experience, we have successfully completed electrical projects up to 132KV across India and internationally, including offshore assignments.

Our commitment to quality, safety, and sustainability drives us to provide innovative solutions that enhance productivity and reliability for our clients. We are proud to be an authorised service center for Larsen & Toubro, offering a wide range of electrical services with a focus on operational excellence.
