Our Story

Building Excellence Since 1985

OK Agencies began its journey in 1985 under the visionary leadership of our founder, Mr. V.A. Oak. What started as a commitment to electrical excellence has evolved into a comprehensive solution provider serving India's leading industries for nearly four decades.

Today, we continue this legacy of excellence under the expert guidance of Mr. Anil Jagtap and Mr. Satish Jagdale, who lead our specialized team in delivering cutting-edge electrical solutions across multiple sectors.
